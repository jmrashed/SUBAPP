LIBRARY INFORMATION

Stamford University Library starts its journey since 2002 and now has become one of the best university libraries in the country. It is first and only university Library in Bangladesh that has achieved ISO 9001:2000 for its quality service. The Library of an Academic institute no doubt can be termed as heart of institute. In order to ensure smooth and optimum use of the library the University authority has introduced various rules and procedure. The rules and regulations are meant to ensure equal access for all to library resources and facilities.
